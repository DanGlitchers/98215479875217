📗 Present Simple

S + V1(s/es)
  • I play football.
    אני משחק כדורגל.
  • She watches TV.
    היא צופה בטלוויזיה.

📙 Present Progressive

S + am/is/are + V-ing
  • I am playing now.
    אני משחק עכשיו.
  • She is watching TV.
    היא צופה עכשיו.

📕 Past Simple

S + V2 / did + V1
  • I played yesterday.
    שחקתי אתמול.
  • Did they watch?
    הם צפו?

📘 Future Simple

S + will + V1
  • I will play tomorrow.
    אשחק מחר.
  • Will they come?
    הם יבואו?

📒 Past Progressive

S + was/were + V-ing
  • I was playing at 5pm.
    שחקתי בשעה 5.
  • They were watching.
    הם צפו.

🎯 טיפים

  • Always, usually → Present Simple
  • Now, at the moment → Present Progressive
  • Yesterday, last week → Past Simple
  • Tomorrow, next year → Future Simple
